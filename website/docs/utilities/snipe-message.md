Retrieves the most recently deleted message in the past hour (non-premium) / 12 hours (premium).

## Trigger

**Type:** `Command`<br />
**Trigger:** `snipe`

## Usage

- `-snipe` - Retrieves the most recently deleted message in the past hour (non-premium) / 12 hours (premium).
